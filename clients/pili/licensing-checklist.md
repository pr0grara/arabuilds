# Pili — CSLB C-8 Licensing Checklist (DRAFT · high-level)

> Internal working doc. High-level skeleton — flesh out into a step-by-step we hand Pili.
> Our role = navigator, not licensing service. Verified specifics in [`discovery-notes.md`](discovery-notes.md) §5.

## The path at a glance
1. **Confirm eligibility** — 4 yrs journey-level experience in last 10 (✓ he has it).
2. **Solve the experience sign-off** — pick who certifies: GC, foreman, fellow journeyman, or another licensed contractor (not necessarily his boss).
3. **File the Original Application** — $450 non-refundable fee → ~4–6 wks to acknowledgment.
4. **Study + pass two exams** — Law & Business + C-8 trade (free CSLB study guides).
5. **Post-exam:** $25k contractor bond, $200 initial license fee (sole owner), Live Scan/background check.
6. **Workers' comp** — mandatory for C-8 even solo; line up before issuance.
7. **License issued** — valid 2 years.

## What we prep vs. what's his
- **Ours:** map the steps, identify the cert path, point to study resources, track deadlines, pre-fill what we can.
- **His:** the exam, fingerprinting, signing the bond, paying fees.
